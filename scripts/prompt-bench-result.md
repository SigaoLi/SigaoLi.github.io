# 提示词对比实验（模型固定：deepseek-v4-pro）

测试句：
1. From maps to models, and the products in between.
2. Three disciplines, one through-line: points, and the patterns between them.
3. A geographer who ships.
4. Unglamorous correctness. Full In-Reply-To/References header maintenance keeps threads intact in every client.
5. Six years of geographic analysis — mapping retail networks, public health and crime across the Toronto CMA, and learning that every location is a decision.
6. Working across languages means every email costs twice: once to read it, once to answer it. Existing clients offer no summarization, no contextual translation, and no memory of who writes what — and switching between a mailbox, a translator and a chat tool breaks flow dozens of times a day.
7. The agent turns a multi-tool, multi-language chore into a three-tap review flow — with a human always in the loop before send.

## V1 创意重写（上轮B）

1. 从地图到模型，以及两者之间的一切产品。
2. 三个领域，一条暗线：点，与点之间的规律。
3. 一个能把东西做出来的地理人。
4. 不酷的正确。完整的 In-Reply-To/References 头维护，让对话线程在任何客户端都不散不乱。
5. 六年地理分析——绘过零售网络，画过公共卫生与犯罪分布，覆盖整个多伦多 CMA，最终学会一件事：每一个位置，都是一个决策。
6. 跨语言工作，意味着每封邮件都要花两遍力气：一遍读懂，一遍回复。现有客户端没有摘要、没有语境翻译、也记不住谁写过什么——在邮箱、翻译工具和聊天软件之间来回切换，一天打断心流几十次。
7. 这个 Agent，把多工具、多语言的繁琐流程，变成三步审核即可发送——而且，在按下发送键之前，人始终在回路里。
